## How to Request a Service

1. Go to [**New Issue**](../../issues/new/choose)
2. Select **📋 Request a Service**
3. Fill in the form — provide your cost center and manager for approval
4. Your request will be automatically routed to the right team
5. Track progress on the [**ITSM Board**](../../projects)

## Escalation

If your request is urgent or not progressing:
- Add a comment tagging `@cloud-ops-team`
- For critical requests, contact the [on-call channel](#) directly
